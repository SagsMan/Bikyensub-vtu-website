Frequently Asked Questions

How do I fund my Bikyensub wallet?

Login to your dashboard, click "Fund Wallet" and transfer via bank or card. Your wallet is credited automatically within seconds.

What networks do you support?

We support MTN, Airtel, GLO, and 9mobile for airtime and data. Cable TV: DSTV, GOTV, Startimes. All Nigerian electricity DISCOs are also supported.

What happens if my transaction fails?

Failed transactions are automatically reversed to your wallet within 1–5 minutes. If not reversed, contact us on WhatsApp with your transaction reference.

How do I become a reseller on Bikyensub?

Register a free account, fund your wallet, then contact us on WhatsApp to upgrade to a reseller/aggregator account. You'll get your own VTU website.

Is Bikyensub safe to use?

Yes. All transactions are SSL encrypted and processed through secure gateways. We've served 155,000+ users since launch with zero data breach incidents.
