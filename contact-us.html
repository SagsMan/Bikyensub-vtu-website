Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I fund my wallet?

Login to your dashboard, click "Fund Wallet" and pay via bank transfer or card. Your wallet is credited automatically.

Why wasn't my data delivered?

Failed transactions are auto-reversed within minutes. If not, contact us on WhatsApp with your transaction reference.

How do I become a reseller?

Register a free account, fund your wallet, and contact us on WhatsApp to upgrade to a reseller account.

What networks are supported?

We support MTN, Airtel, GLO, and 9mobile for airtime and data. Cable TV: DSTV, GOTV, Startimes.

Is my payment information safe?

Yes. All transactions are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways with SSL protection.

Can I get a developer API?

Yes! We provide VTU API for developers. Contact us on WhatsApp to get your API credentials and documentation.
